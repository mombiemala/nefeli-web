# NEFELI — Market Research (July 2026)

Evidence-based validation of the "whole-self astrology companion" direction.
Produced from a multi-source, adversarially-verified research pass. Prefer recent
(2024–2026) sources. Where the evidence was thin, that is stated plainly.

## Bottom line

**Demand is not the risk — retention and differentiation are.** The adjacent
AI-companion market is real and monetizing fast, and the emotional-tone gap
NEFELI targets is real and demonstrable. But it is a winner-take-most market
where AI apps churn faster, so the vision holds *only if* first-session value and
a safe, non-fatalistic voice are treated as the core product, not polish.

## What the evidence validates

**The market is real and paying (AI companions, the adjacent category).**
- ~$82M revenue H1 2025, on track for **$120M+ full-year 2025**; 60M downloads in
  H1 (+88% YoY); 220M cumulative by July 2025.
- Revenue-per-download **more than doubled, $0.52 → $1.18** — rising willingness
  to pay. — *Appfigures via TechCrunch, Aug 2025.*

**The emotional-tone gap is real.** Co-Star has a well-documented reputation for
harsh, morbid, "negging" copy that press and users criticize as preying on mental
health. A warm, non-bypassing companion is a concrete, citable wedge.

**Astrology × fashion is an executable, editorially-proven concept** (e.g. ELLE's
per-sign outfit forecasts) — *but see the pivot: we are not pursuing it.*

## The sobering part — retention benchmarks

- Only **~27–28% of annual subscribers renew after one year**, nearly independent
  of paywall model (28% freemium vs 27% hard paywall).
- AI apps: **+41% revenue per payer but ~30% faster churn.** Monetization isn't
  the problem; keeping people is.
- **Winner-take-most:** top 25% of apps grew +80% YoY while the bottom 25% shrank
  −33% (median +5.3%). Top 10% of companion apps capture ~89% of revenue.
- **First session decides it:** a large share of trial cancellations happen Day 0;
  longer trials convert markedly better. → onboarding must deliver a real "wow."
  — *RevenueCat State of Subscription Apps 2026; a16z 100 Gen AI Apps (6th ed.).*

## Risks / headwinds

- **AI-companion safety scrutiny is intensifying** — 2025 Senate letters to
  Character.AI / Replika / Chai; wrongful-harm lawsuits involving minors. Anything
  that discusses a user's inner life inherits this. Safe tone + guardrails are
  table stakes.
- **Platform / distribution risk** — Co-Star was pulled from Google Play at one
  point. Keep the web app central; don't depend on one channel.
- **Intellectual-honesty caveats.** The research did **not** surface direct
  evidence that *persistent memory / cross-domain life integration* is a proven
  unmet *need* (compelling, but our bet — prove it via visible payoff), nor clean
  astrology-specific market-size / willingness-to-pay figures.
